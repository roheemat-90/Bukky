THE DOs AND DON'Ts IN FASHION
S/N The Dos The Don'ts
1. Keep your budget in mind at all times Fashion is an expensivse addiction a person can get into. If money is not an issue, then indulging in designer brands from time to time should not be an issue. However, if you are on a tight budget and unfortunately exceed your budget, then you are setting yourself up for failure
2. Monotone Outfits More and more people are choosing to wear single color for their outfit. Black is the most popular color women choose because of its classic and chic look.
3. Look the part Women take great pride in the way they look, especially when it comes to an important event. Many women put in a lot of thought and time when it comes to putting together a complete outfit. You can visit other websites to gain outfit inspiration, as well as tge opportunity to shop quality clothing at affordable prices. For example, when attending a formal wedding, floor-length dresses, cocktail dresses, and Jumpsuits are appropriate attire for the event.
